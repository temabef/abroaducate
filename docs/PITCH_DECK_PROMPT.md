# Abroaducate: Pitch Deck Creation Prompt

This document provides a **comprehensive platform summary** and a **ready-to-use prompt** you can give to a designer, copywriter, or AI tool (e.g., ChatGPT, Claude, or a pitch deck generator) to create a professional pitch deck for Abroaducate.

## Part 1: Platform Summary (for context)

### What Abroaducate is (one sentence)

**Abroaducate is an AI-powered study-abroad platform that guides students through a single journey: get a free roadmap (diagnostic), complete profile/onboarding, then use tools (SOP, scholarships, university matching, test prep, applications) with optional premium upgrades.**

### Core value proposition

- **"You never pay to browse. You pay to decide."**  
  Users don’t pay for more listings; they pay for **prioritized ranking**, **reasoning and confidence**, **clear next steps**, and **deadlines/sequencing**. The platform sells clarity and time savings, not just access to data.

- **Headline:** “Get your study abroad plan in minutes.”  
  Start with a **free Plan** (quick diagnostic), then get your **Strategy Pack**: a ranked shortlist (scholarships + universities), step-by-step guidance, and a simple timeline—built from the user’s profile.

### Target audience

- **Primary:** International students (undergraduate, master’s, PhD) planning to study abroad in the US, UK, Canada, Australia, Germany, Netherlands, and other major destinations.
- **Secondary:** Education consultants, advisors, and high-volume applicants who need document generation and application tracking at scale.

### Key features (what the platform does)

| Category | Features |
|----------|----------|
| **Entry & discovery** | Single CTA “Get my free plan” → diagnostic; university/program finder (destination, level, field); browse scholarships and universities for free. |
| **Strategy & matching** | **Strategy Pack**: ranked scholarships, safety/target/reach university plan, application timeline, “next best actions”; unified profile drives all matching. |
| **Documents (AI)** | Statement of Purpose (SOP), Cover Letters, Personal Statements, Academic CVs; AI review, text enhancement, word optimization; tiered limits (e.g. free: 4 docs/month; Professional: 50; Elite: unlimited). |
| **Search & match** | 1,500+ universities; 500+ scholarships; GPA conversion (40+ international systems); university matching with compatibility scoring; scholarship match scores. |
| **Prep & track** | Free IELTS test prep (Reading, Listening, Writing, Speaking); application tracking; document checklists; deadline reminders; budget calculator; cold-email generator (paid). |
| **Monetization** | Free tier (browse + basic limits) → **Strategist / Academic Professional** (~$10–12/mo: full Strategy Pack, more docs, advanced matching) → **Advisor / Academic Elite** (~$25–30/mo: unlimited docs, 1000+ universities, premium support). |

### Business model & positioning

- **Free:** Browse all scholarships and universities; basic match scores; limited document generation and AI features; application tracking.
- **Paid (Strategist / Professional):** Full ranked Strategy Pack with explainability, safety/target/reach plan, personalized timeline, smart reminders; more documents and AI usage; 100–300+ universities; priority support.
- **Premium (Advisor / Elite):** Unlimited documents, 1000+ universities, GPT-4-level AI, advanced cold email, team/collaboration options; human advisory/mentorship in roadmap.

**Differentiation vs. alternatives:**

- **vs. traditional consultants:** $500–$3,000+ per engagement → Abroaducate offers 24/7 AI + tools from ~$12/mo, with a strong free tier.
- **vs. scholarship/listing sites:** Commoditized “more listings” → Abroaducate focuses on **decision intelligence**: ranked shortlist, reasoning, timeline, next actions.
- **Message:** “We reduce 40 hours of uncertainty to a 30-minute plan.”

### Tech & traction (for “how it’s built” / credibility)

- **Stack:** SvelteKit 5, Supabase (auth, DB), Stripe (subscriptions), OpenAI (GPT), TailwindCSS.
- **Scale:** 50+ routes, 70+ components, 13+ services; production-ready; usage limits and paywalls in place.
- **Roadmap:** Strategy Pack as core monetizable product; timeline/reminders; human advisory tier; university/scholarship DB expansion.

### Optional stats to cite (if available)

- “Trusted by students from 50+ countries” (or current user geography).
- 1,500+ universities, 500+ scholarships, 40+ grading systems.
- Free IELTS test prep; unlimited for all users.

### **PROMPT START**

Create a professional pitch deck for **Abroaducate**, an AI-powered study-abroad platform. Use the following structure and content guidelines. Aim for **10–14 slides**; keep copy short and scannable.

**1. Title slide**  
- **Abroaducate** — tagline: “Your AI study abroad guide” or “Get your study abroad plan in minutes.”  
- Optional subtitle: “Ranked shortlist + timeline — free to start.”

**2. Problem**  
- International students face **information overload and decision paralysis**: thousands of universities and scholarships, unclear fit, scattered deadlines, and expensive consultants ($500–$3,000+).  
- Time spent: 40+ hours of research and guesswork with no clear “what to do next.”

**3. Solution**  
- One platform: **free diagnostic → Strategy Pack (ranked scholarships + universities + timeline) → AI documents (SOP, CV, etc.) → test prep & application tracking.**  
- Core message: “You never pay to browse. You pay to decide.” We turn 40 hours of uncertainty into a 30-minute plan.

**4. Product (how it works)**  
- **Step 1:** User takes a short diagnostic (degree, field, GPA, countries).  
- **Step 2:** Get a **Strategy Pack**: ranked scholarships, safety/target/reach universities, application timeline, next best actions.  
- **Step 3:** Use AI tools for SOP, cover letters, personal statements, CVs; track applications; prep for IELTS—all in one place.  
- Emphasize: one profile, one dashboard, one journey (no juggling multiple tools).

**5. Key features (visual grid or icons)**  
- Strategy Pack (ranked shortlist + explainability + timeline).  
- AI document generation (SOP, CV, cover letter, personal statement).  
- University matching (1,500+ universities) & scholarship matching (500+).  
- Application tracking, checklists, reminders.  
- Free IELTS test prep; GPA converter (40+ systems); budget calculator.  
- Optional: cold-email generator, document collaboration (roadmap).

**6. Business model**  
- **Free:** Browse everything; basic match scores; limited docs/AI.  
- **Strategist / Professional (~$10–12/mo):** Full Strategy Pack, more documents, advanced matching, reminders.  
- **Advisor / Elite (~$25–30/mo):** Unlimited docs, 1000+ universities, premium AI, optional human advisory.  
- Monetization principle: charge for **decision clarity and execution**, not for browsing.

**7. Market opportunity**  
- Global study-abroad market: millions of students annually (cite a recent stat if available, e.g. 6M+ international students).  
- TAM/SAM/SOM: students applying to US, UK, Canada, Australia, Germany, etc.; segment by degree level (bachelor’s, master’s, PhD) and by willingness to pay for guidance and documents.

**8. Competition / differentiation**  
- **Traditional consultants:** Expensive, one-off; we offer always-on AI + tools at a fraction of the cost.  
- **Scholarship/listing sites:** Commoditized access; we offer ranked shortlist, reasoning, and timeline (decision intelligence).  
- **Standout:** All-in-one (diagnostic → plan → documents → track) with a strong free tier to demonstrate value.

**9. Traction (if any)**  
- Use placeholders if needed: “Users from 50+ countries,” “1,500+ universities, 500+ scholarships,” “Free tier drives signups; conversion to paid for Strategy Pack and documents.”  
- Replace with real metrics (signups, conversions, MRR, retention) when available.

**10. Team**  
- [Insert: 1–3 lines on founding team background—education, edtech, AI, or international experience.]

**11. Roadmap**  
- Near-term: Strategy Pack as core product; timeline & reminders; improved upgrade prompts.  
- Mid-term: Human advisory/mentorship tier; more universities/scholarships; engagement (e.g. success stories, notifications).  
- Long-term: Partnerships (universities, counselors); expansion to more countries and degree types.

**12. Ask**  
- [Insert: funding amount and use of funds, e.g. product, marketing, operations.]  
- Or: “We’re raising [X] to scale user acquisition and solidify the Strategy Pack as the category-defining study-abroad decision engine.”

**13. Closing / contact**  
- Abroaducate — “Get your study abroad plan in minutes.”  
- Website, email, and/or Calendly for meetings.

**Design tone:** Clean, modern, trustworthy; suitable for investors and institutional partners. Use a consistent color palette (e.g. primary blue/navy such as #2C3580 if matching the product); avoid clutter; one main idea per slide.

**PROMPT END**
