What's working

The replies. Every email I've sent has generated real conversations. People telling me about their situation, asking for specific recommendations, sharing what they're struggling with. That's the most valuable signal I have.

The scholarship matching is also resonating. The most common thing people say when they find the platform is that they didn't know this many scholarships existed, or that they didn't know how to find the ones relevant to their specific program. That's exactly the problem I built it to solve.

What's not working yet

Getting people from browsing to actually using the AI tools. Most people who visit the platform look at programs and scholarships but don't generate a document or run a strategy. I think part of this is trust — people aren't sure what the output will look like — and part of it is that the free credit isn't visible enough.

I'm working on making the first experience clearer. If you've been on the platform and didn't try the AI tools, I'd genuinely like to know why. Reply and tell me.
