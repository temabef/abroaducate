## 🎯 **System Overview**

### **Core Functionality:**
The Personal Statement Generator creates compelling, story-driven personal narratives that differentiate from our academic Statement of Purpose tool. This system serves **6 distinct application types** with specialized prompts and guidance.

### **Supported Application Types:**

#### 1. **🎓 Undergraduate Applications**
- **Target**: High school students applying to colleges
- **Focus**: Character development, academic curiosity, potential
- **Tone**: Conversational yet thoughtful
- **Structure**: Personal growth → Challenges → Academic engagement → Future goals
- **Word Limits**: 250-1000 words

#### 2. **🏆 Scholarship Applications**
- **Target**: Students seeking financial aid
- **Focus**: Personal journey, need/merit justification, gratitude
- **Tone**: Humble yet confident
- **Structure**: Background → Financial context → Impact → Future giving
- **Word Limits**: 250-1000 words

#### 3. **⚖️ Law School Applications**
- **Target**: Pre-law students
- **Focus**: Justice interest, analytical thinking, ethical principles
- **Tone**: Professional yet personal
- **Structure**: Legal spark → Analytical skills → Professional understanding → Career vision
- **Word Limits**: 500-1500 words

#### 4. **🏥 Medical School Applications**
- **Target**: Pre-med students
- **Focus**: Calling to heal, service experiences, empathy
- **Tone**: Sincere, service-oriented
- **Structure**: Medical calling → Service experiences → Professional demands → Compassionate care
- **Word Limits**: 500-1500 words

#### 5. **🌍 Study Abroad Applications**
- **Target**: Students seeking international experience
- **Focus**: Cultural curiosity, global perspective, adaptability
- **Tone**: Adventurous yet respectful
- **Structure**: Cultural interests → Diversity experiences → Program appeal → Growth goals
- **Word Limits**: 250-1000 words

#### 6. **💼 Professional Programs**
- **Target**: Working professionals, MBA candidates
- **Focus**: Career progression, leadership, business impact
- **Tone**: Professional yet authentic
- **Structure**: Career journey → Leadership examples → Program fit → Professional vision
- **Word Limits**: 500-1500 words
