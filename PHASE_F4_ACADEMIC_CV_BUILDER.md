- **6-Step Process:** Template → Personal Info → Education → Research & Publications → Experience → Awards & Skills → Review & Generate
- **Field-Specific Templates:** 6 academic field templates with tailored sections and guidance
- **Progress Tracking:** Visual progress bar and step navigation
- **Smart Validation:** Step-by-step validation ensuring completeness

### 2. **Field-Specific Templates**
```
🔬 STEM Fields - Research, publications, technical skills emphasis
📚 Humanities - Teaching, languages, scholarly engagement focus  
🌍 Social Sciences - Research + community engagement balance
💼 Business & Economics - Practical experience + academic credentials

🎨 Arts & Creative - Portfolio, exhibitions, creative collaborations
```

### 3. **Comprehensive Academic Sections**
- **Personal Information:** Contact details, professional links, ORCID
- **Education:** Degrees, institutions, GPA, thesis titles, advisors
- **Research Experience:** Projects, institutions, supervisors, descriptions
- **Publications:** Journal articles, conference papers, books, preprints
- **Professional Experience:** Teaching, industry, volunteer, internships
- **Awards & Honors:** Academic awards, fellowships, recognition
- **Skills & Competencies:** Technical skills, languages, software tools

### 4. **Smart Content Management**
- **Dynamic Sections:** Add/remove entries for each section
- **Flexible Input:** Optional fields for comprehensive or minimal CVs
- **Pre-population:** Integration with existing user profile data
- **Custom Instructions:** Additional formatting and style preferences
